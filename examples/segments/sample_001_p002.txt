"Five silver for a bundle!" a merchant called out as she passed. Alex barely registered his voice among the cacophony of the market. Her focus was entirely on the small stall at the far end, where an old woman sat behind a table covered with trinkets and oddities.

As she approached, the old woman looked up and smiled, revealing a row of surprisingly white teeth. "I've been expecting you," she said.

Alex raised an eyebrow. "People usually say that after I introduce myself."
